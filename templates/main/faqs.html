Libre po ba ang lahat?

Yes, pero madaming miscellaneous (e.g. projects, ambangan)

May intramurals po ba kayo?

Yes we have, although sports are not the school's priority.

English
Filipino
Mathematics
Araling Panlipunan
Science

Music, Arts, Physical Education and Health (MAPEH)

For Senior High School:

Pwede po ba magtransfer from other schools to QSHS?

Pwede, pero dapat galing sa ibang science high school

Pwede po ba mag Senior High School (SHS) sa QSHS kahit nag-Junior High School sa iba?

Nope, only Grade 10 students in our school can enroll to our Senior High School (SHS) Program.

Anong mga strands ang ino-offer ng QSHS for Senior High School?

Our school only offers STEM track

Sino lang po pwede mag-dorm?

Open for all students, pero mas prioritized ang mga nakatira sa malalayong bayan.

May free internet po ba sa QSHS?

No, we prefer our students to provide their own source.

Pwede po bang bumisita ang mga magulang sa dorm?

Yes, pero may allowed time lang.

Madalas ba ang activities sa school nyo?

Yes, there are times na madaming events due to multple club activities na nagkakasabay sabay

May clubs po ba sa school nyo?

Meron, in fact we have 12 established organizations. Learn more here

Kailangan po ba na magaling sa Science and/or Math para maging honor student?

Yes, because we always aim for academic excellence especially in the field of Science, Mathematics and Technology
